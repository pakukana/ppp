Piha is a seaside village located in west of Auckland, 1h drive from CBD Auckland. There is black-sand beach on coast. It is one of the famous and beautiful beaches in Auckland.The view from the beach is amazing!

Lion-rock is a natural formation dividing North and South Piha beaches. It is an eroded 16-million-year-old volcanic neck, named for its similarity to a lying male lion when viewed from the rear (shore side), Lion Rock is immediately visible to visitors as they descend along the only access road. Lion Rock has become iconic not only of Piha, but of Auckland's West Coast in general.(wikipedia)
